Median Salary of Bermudian Government Workers

The median salary of a Bermudian Government worker is $82k. At the top of the pay scale are some of the most senior officials in the government. The Premier, Financial Secretary, and Governor are among the top earners, with salaries ranging from $207k to $224k. On the other end of the spectrum, the lowest-paid positions are often those that require less education or experience, such as Cooks, Receptionists and Clerks, with annual salaries as low as $50k.

Pay Scales Across Ministries

While some ministries pay higher salaries than others, there is a wide range of pay scales across all ministries. For instance, the Department of Public prosecutions pays the highest median salary at $119k, while the Youth, Culture and Sport ministry pays the lowest median salary at $60k. Other top-paying ministries include Health, Home Affairs, and Legal Affairs and Constitutional Reform.

It's important to note that these are median salaries, meaning that some government workers within a given ministry may earn more or less than the stated amount. Additionally, these salaries are gross annual incomes, and do not take into account any deductions for taxes or social security contributions.
